I'm a front-end software engineer, educator, gardener, and gamer.

I'm a developer with about 5 years of experience in the tech industry. Consultant roles early on in my career have afforded me the advantage of gaining experience in a multitude of roles and environments that have made me adaptable. In an ever evolving tech landscape, developers must be flexible enough to learn and use the right tools for any given job, but also need a solid foundation to build upon. This is my greatest strength.

My technical strengths have a foundation based in JavaScript/TypeScript, but as my career opportunities have expanded, so has my skillset. After attending a bootcamp and learning React, I have added Vue.js, Angular.js, Node.js, and other JS/TS based frameworks/libraries to my repertoire. Currently, I am in the process of learning Python and C#.

The pursuit of knowledge is at the foundation of my career pursuits as well as my personal hobbies. I believe knowledge is invaluable and one cannot limit the places that they seek it. I'm passionate about continuously building my mental fortitude via collaboration with others and my natural curiosity.
